ABOUT "FAITES DU SON !"

"Faites du son !" is a music technology fair (in French) happening on June 9-12, 2022 at the Comète in Saint-Étienne as part of SMC-22. It will gather 24 workshops around sound, music, and programming, as well as a series of concerts and conferences targeted towards the general public and local schools.

Program

Thursday, June 9, 2022

Professional day around digital and experimental music consisting of round tables and meetings, brainstorm sessions on the teaching of arts and science, etc. (professional event closed to the public)

Friday, June 10

Day dedicated to local schools (cycle 3 to cycle 4)

Saturday and Sunday, June 11-12

workshops opened to the public (registration mandatory)
