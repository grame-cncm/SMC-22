ABOUT SMC 2022

SMC-22 (Sound and Music Computing) is a multifaceted event around acoustics, music, and audio technology. It will take place in Saint-Étienne (France) on June 4-12, 2022 and will have the following theme: Music Technology and Design. As the first "in person" edition of SMC after 2 years of COVID-19 pandemic, we aim at widening the public of this event by (i) broadening the scope of SMC concerts through a digital art festival opened to the public, (ii) involving local schools and publics, and (iii) strengthening connections between academia and the industry.

SMC-22 will be a combination of a Summer School (June 5-7, 2022), the International Faust Conference (June 7-8, 2022), the SMC Scientific Conference, Industry-Oriented Events, a Festival (June 8-11, 2022), and a STEAM (Science Technology Engineering Arts and Mathematics) Faire (June 9-12).

SMC-22 is organized by the ECLLA Lab at Université Jean Monnet of Saint-Étienne, GRAME - Centre National de Création Musicale, INRIA, and ARCAN, in collaboration with various local partners such as the city and the métropole of Saint-Étienne.

SCIENTIFIC CONFERENCES

International Faust Conference (IFC-22)

The International Faust Conference (IFC) gathers members of the Faust programming language community every other year. It will take place at Centre des savoirs pour l'innovation of Saint-Étienne University on June 7-8, 2022 and will feature talks and demos around Faust. The IFC-22 Call for Papers (CfP) is accessible here.


SMC Conference

The SMC scientific conference will take place at Télécom Saint-Étienne on June 8-11, 2022. It will feature poster presentations, demos, lightening talks, and keynotes. The SMC-22 Call for Papers (CfP) is accessible here.


Journées de l'Informatique Musicale (JIM-22)

In 2022, the Journées de l'Informatique Musicale will be merged with the SMC conference. A half day special session featuring oral presentations in French by young researchers will be organized in parallel of the SMC main track. A specific Call for Papers (CfP) for this kind of submissions is accessible here.

SMC FESTIVAL

The SMC Festival will feature installations and over ten concerts in multiple venues throughout the city of Saint-Étienne, offering a wide range of settings for various kinds of music and medias (e.g., exploratory, orchestral, jazz, pop, digital art, etc.): clubs, churches, opera house, factories, concert halls, etc. Professional ensembles such as the Ensemble Orchestral Contemporain (EOC) will be mobilized and musicians from Saint-Étienne conservatory will be made available for performances. The SMC-22 Call for Art (CfA) can be seen here. It includes a contest for having a piece performed by the EOC at Saint-Étienne Opera House.

SUMMER SCHOOL

The SMC Summer School will take place at the Comète on June 5-7, 2022. It will consist in four one day workshops given by prestigious members of the SMC community and will follow the general theme of SMC-22 on "Music Technology and Design." A total of 25 grad students will be selected to participate (for free) to the SMC Summer School.

STEAM FAIRE

The SMC STEAM Faire will take place at the Comète on June 9-12, 2022. It will feature a wide range of free workshops, talks, demos, etc. for local schools and the general public around music, science, and technology.

INDUSTRY TALKS

Half a day of the SMC conference will be dedicated to talks by engineers and researchers in the industry. A special industry Call for Presentations can be seen here.
