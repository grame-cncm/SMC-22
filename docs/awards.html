SMC-22 Awards

Best Paper Award

The SMC-22 Best Paper Award has been awarded to Claire Richards, Roland Cahen, and Nicolas Misdariis for their paper Designing the balance between sound and touch: Methods for multimodal composition. They received a 500 euros cash price (kindly provided by the AFIM) as well as a Bela Mini Kit.

Best Presentation Award

The SMC-22 Best Presentation Award has been awarded to Yann Seznec and Sandra Pauletto for their paper The Singing Shower: A Melody-Sensitive Interface for Physical Interaction and Efficient Energy Consumption. They received a 250 euros cash price (kindly provided by the AFIM) as well as a Bela Trill Kit.

JIM Young Researcher Award

The SMC-22 JIM Young Researcher Award has been awarded to Alice Sauda, Mathieu Giraud, and Emmanuel Leguy for their paper Soutenir en classe l'écoute active, l'autonomie et l'échange en analyse musicale avec la plateforme web Dezrann. They received a 250 euros cash price (kindly provided by the AFIM) as well as a Bela Trill Kit.
