SMC-22 STEAM Faire
June 9-12, 2022
Saint-Etienne
(France)

ABOUT THE SMC-22 Steam Faire

The SMC-22 STEAM Faire will take place on June 9-12, 2022 at the Comète. It will feature a wide range of workshops, demos, and talks around art, technology, and sciences by local and national partners. It will target local schools as well as the general public to raise awareness around the SMC fields.

THE COMÈTE HALL

The Comète Entrance Hall will host the SMC STEAM Faire. Various rooms of the Comète will be used as well to organize workshops, etc.
