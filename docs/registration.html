SMC-22 Registration Information

How to Register?

Registration to SMC-22 will open at the end of Winter 2022. Stay tuned!

Registration Fees

Authors with accepted papers will have the option to attend the conference in person or remotely. The registration fee for this category of participants includes everything (i.e., banquet ticket, special concerts, publication, etc.). Free auditors (people who are not presenting a paper) will be able to attend SMC-22 for free. Artists with an accepted piece to the SMC-22 Festival will not have to pay anything, of course, unless they also have an accepted paper.
